How to get your usage data & Rate Plan Info

How to export your data from Georgia Power:

  1. Log in to your Georgia Power account and go to Billing and Payments > Usage.

  2. Select Hourly view and toggle to Table mode.

  3. Click the Export button below the table.

  4. Select Custom Date Range. Go back at least 1 year (up to 2 years) for the best results. Click Export.
